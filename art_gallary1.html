Ocean Dreams

Watercolor techniques were used to give this piece a fluid, dream-like quality.
It reminds me of summer mornings by the sea.

Steps to Paint:

  1. Sketch the outline lightly.
  2. Wet the paper before applying paint.
  3. Layer different blues for depth.
  4. Let it dry completely.
